How It Works

Our pneumonia detection system utilizes a state-of-the-art deep learning model trained on a comprehensive dataset of chest X-ray images. The model employs convolutional neural networks (CNNs) to analyze the structural patterns and abnormalities in lung tissue that are characteristic of pneumonia.


The backend architecture processes uploaded chest X-ray scans through multiple layers of feature extraction and classification. Each image undergoes preprocessing to normalize lighting conditions and enhance relevant features before being fed into the neural network for analysis.


Our system provides rapid, automated screening that can assist healthcare professionals in making faster diagnostic decisions, potentially reducing the time to treatment initiation.
